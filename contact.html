Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do online classes work?

Our classes are conducted via video call with professional teachers. You will receive a link before each session and can join from any device.

What if I miss a class?

We understand life happens. You can reschedule classes with 24-hour notice at no extra charge.

Do I need prior Arabic knowledge?

Not at all. We have courses for complete beginners who have never studied Arabic before.

How quickly can I see results?

Most students start reading and speaking basic Arabic within the first month of consistent practice.
